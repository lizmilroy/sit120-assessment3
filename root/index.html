Keyboard Building Service

Welcome to my website! My name is Liz, and I create mechanical keyboards. Building your own mechanical keyboard can be time-consuming and challenging, so allow me to make it easy for you!

It's as simple as telling me what you want! I will then get the parts, build, and ship the keyboard to you. If you're a beginner, don't worry! This website has lots of information about mechanical keyboards. You can find informtion about everything needed to place an order.

If you know exactly what you want, head over to the Order page and get started on building your keyboard. If you're not sure, have a look at the FAQ page. This will provide you with all the information you need.
